Consumer Behavior Maslow Quiz

1. A company advertises a new line of organic foods with the slogan “Purely Natural, Purely You.” Which need from Maslow’s hierarchy is this campaign targeting?

Correct Answer: B

Explanation: The campaign focuses on organic foods, which relate to basic physiological needs like food and health.

2. A car company promotes its vehicles with the tagline “Drive with Confidence.” Which need is this targeting?

Correct Answer: B

Explanation: The tagline emphasizes confidence in driving, which relates to safety needs by promoting security and protection.

3. A cosmetics brand uses the slogan “Be Your Beautiful Self.” Which need is this campaign addressing?

Correct Answer: C

Explanation: The slogan focuses on beauty and self-expression, which ties to esteem needs by enhancing self-worth and confidence.

4. A travel agency advertises with “Discover Your True Potential.” Which need is this targeting?

Correct Answer: C

Explanation: The focus on discovering potential aligns with self-actualization needs, emphasizing personal growth and fulfillment.

5. A fast-food chain uses bright red and yellow colors in its packaging. Which psychological factor is this targeting?

Correct Answer: B

Explanation: Bright colors influence perception by attracting attention and creating a sense of excitement through visual stimuli.

6. A gym promotes memberships with “Join the Fitness Family.” Which need is this targeting?

Correct Answer: C

Explanation: The slogan emphasizes community and connection, appealing to belongingness needs.

7. A luxury car brand uses the tagline “Engineered for Excellence.” Which need is this targeting?

Correct Answer: C

Explanation: The focus on excellence appeals to esteem needs, emphasizing status and achievement.

8. A tech company promotes its products with “Innovate the Future.” Which psychological factor is this campaign targeting to create a positive attitude?

Correct Answer: B

Explanation: The slogan links the brand to innovation, a concept people generally view positively, to shape favorable consumer attitudes.

9. An insurance company advertises with “Protect What Matters Most.” Which need is this targeting?

Correct Answer: B

Explanation: The slogan emphasizes protection, which aligns with safety needs in Maslow’s hierarchy.

10. A coffee brand uses a warm, inviting store design to attract customers. Which psychological factor is this targeting?

Correct Answer: B

Explanation: The store design influences perception by creating a positive sensory experience through sight and ambiance.

11. A motivational seminar promotes “Achieve Your Dreams.” Which need is this targeting?

Correct Answer: D

Explanation: The focus on achieving dreams aligns with self-actualization needs, emphasizing personal growth and fulfillment.

12. A clothing brand promotes its eco-friendly products with “Wear the Change for a Greener Planet.” Which psychological factor is this campaign targeting to create a positive attitude?

14. A tech company uses sleek, modern packaging for its products. Which psychological factor is this targeting?
