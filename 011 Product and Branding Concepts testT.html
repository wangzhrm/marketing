Contemporary Marketing, 20e

Chapter 11: Product and Branding Concepts
Interactive Multiple Choice Quiz

1. Which of the following best defines goods according to the chapter?
Correct Answer: B) Tangible products that customers can see, hear, smell, taste, and/or touch
2. Which characteristic of services means they cannot be separated from the service provider?
Correct Answer: C) Inseparability
3. On the goods–services continuum, where would a smartphone be placed?
Correct Answer: C) Mostly goods with accompanying services
4. Which of the following is classified as a convenience product?
Correct Answer: B) A pack of gum purchased at checkout
5. Shopping products are typically characterized by:
Correct Answer: B) High price and comparison shopping by consumers
6. Which type of consumer product requires extensive planning time and very little comparison of price and quality?
Correct Answer: C) Specialty products
7. Installations in the B2B market refer to:
Correct Answer: B) Major capital investments such as factories or heavy machinery
8. Which of the following is an example of component parts and materials?
Correct Answer: B) Tires installed on a new car during manufacturing
9. The highest level of brand loyalty is known as:
Correct Answer: C) Brand insistence
10. Which of the following is NOT one of the four dimensions of brand personality?
Correct Answer: D) Profitability
11. Brand equity refers to:
Correct Answer: B) The added value a brand gives to a product in the marketplace
12. A trademark legally protects:
Correct Answer: B) The brand name, brand mark, and any slogan or abbreviation
13. Which of the following best describes trade dress?
Correct Answer: B) Visual cues that create an overall look differentiating a brand
14. Private brands (private labels) are:
Correct Answer: B) Offered by wholesalers and retailers
15. A brand extension involves:
Correct Answer: B) Using an existing brand name on a new product in an unrelated category
16. Who is responsible for a single brand and develops its marketing strategy, pricing, and promotion?
Correct Answer: C) Brand manager
17. Category management is a system where a manager oversees:
Correct Answer: B) Multiple brands within a single product category
18. One of the three major objectives of packaging is:
Correct Answer: B) Assistance in marketing the product
19. Which legislation requires detailed nutritional information on food labels?
Correct Answer: B) Nutrition Labeling and Education Act of 1990
20. Tamper-resistant packaging is primarily designed to achieve which packaging objective?
Correct Answer: C) Protection against damage, spoilage, and pilferage

End of Interactive Quiz
