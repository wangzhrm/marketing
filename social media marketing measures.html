Choosing the right metrics is essential for accurate campaign evaluation.

1. Increase Brand Awareness üó£Ô∏è

This goal focuses on increasing the visibility and recognition of your brand.

Suitable Measures:

  • Reach: The total number of unique people who saw your content.
  • Impressions: The total number of times your content was displayed.
  • Follower Count/Growth Rate: The increase in your total number of followers.
  • Mentions and Tags: How often your brand is mentioned by other users.

2. Drive Engagement and Community Building ü§ù

This goal aims to encourage interaction and foster a loyal community around your brand.

Suitable Measures:

  • Likes and Reactions: The number of positive responses to your content.
  • Comments: The number of user discussions generated by your content.
  • Shares and Retweets: How often your content is passed on to other networks.
  • Engagement Rate: The percentage of your audience that interacts with your content.

3. Generate Website Traffic or Leads üîó

This goal is centered on driving users from social media to an external site or collecting contact information.

Suitable Measures:

  • Click-Through Rate (CTR): The percentage of people who click on your link.
  • Referral Traffic: The number of website visits coming directly from social media.
  • Cost Per Click (CPC): The amount you pay for each click on a paid ad.
  • Lead Conversion Rate: The number of social media visitors who complete a desired action (e.g., sign-up).

4. Increase Sales and Conversions üí∞

This is the ultimate goal for many businesses: converting social media activity into revenue.

Suitable Measures:

  • Conversion Rate: The percentage of users who make a purchase or complete a key action.
  • Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): The revenue generated for every dollar spent on ads.
  • Social Media Revenue: The total sales revenue attributed to social media campaigns.
  • Customer Lifetime Value (CLV): The projected net profit from a customer over the entire relationship.
