SunnyBrew Co.

Real-World Company Scenario

Company Overview

SunnyBrew Co. is a mid-sized coffee chain with 80 stores across 5 major cities (e.g., New York, Chicago, Seattle, Austin, Miami).

Current Menu: Espresso-based drinks, cold brew, drip coffee, iced teas, pastries, muffins, sandwiches, and salads.

Customers:

  • Office workers aged 25–40
  • University students
  • Health-conscious millennials
  • Families on weekends

Recent Challenges:

  • Sales growth slowed from +12% to +3% last year
  • Customers complain about long wait times and lack of grab-and-go options
  • Competitors are launching functional drinks (e.g., collagen coffee, mushroom lattes, CBD cold brew)
  • Growing demand for plant-based, eco-friendly packaging, and personalized products

CEO Goal: Launch a new product to increase revenue by 15% within 12 months.
